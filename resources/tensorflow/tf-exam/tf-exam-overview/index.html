    Udemy

    In my honest opinion, the Zero to Mastery TensorFlow for Deep Learning is one of the courses that the instructor has done an incredible job in delivering such details of learning material. There are more than 60 hours of videos coding by doing instead of showing some finished codes and explaining stuff. While watching, he will explain why he code in this way, giving some tips and tricks, and more. He also has an online notebook with all his codes made publicly available for everyone. All that he has taught and coded are in his course are in those notebooks, in detail. Although, I have made a shorter version of mine with lesser details, but still following along with his codes.

    TensorFlow Official Tutorial

    TensorFlow has a lot of tutorials with detailed explanations on their website. I have followed along with their tutorials and made a duplication on Pycharm.

    Here are a few of the tutorial in .py version that I was able to run on my laptop

    • Multi-Class Image Classification with Fashion MNIST dataset
    • Binary-Class Text Classification with IMDB dataset
    • Neural Network Regression with Titanic dataset

    There are other problems that I couldn't run on a local Machine and had to use Google Colab instead.
